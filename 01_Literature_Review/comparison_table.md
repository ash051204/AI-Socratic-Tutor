## Comparative Analysis

The following table summarises the ten reviewed papers across key dimensions relevant to the proposed system:

| Paper / Year | Method / Approach | Dataset / Domain | Question Type | Adaptive / Bloom's | Key Limitation | Relevance |
|-------------|------------------|------------------|---------------|--------------------|----------------|-----------|
| AQG & Answer Assessment Survey (2021) | Survey (Rule + Neural) | SQuAD, NewsQA | Factual, Conceptual | No | Narrow dataset; no difficulty control | High – taxonomy |
| Survey of AQG Approaches (2021) | Rule, Template, Neural | Open-domain | Factual, Inferential | No | No difficulty modelling; redundant questions | High – method overview |
| AQG Review (2023) | Transformer (BERT, T5, GPT-2) | Educational text | Factual, Conceptual | Partial | Evaluation metrics inadequate for pedagogy | Very High |
| AQG Using PLMs (2024) | Fine-tuned T5, GPT variants | STEM, curated edu data | Objective-aligned | Partial | Domain-specific; limited generalisation | Very High |
| QA Pair Generation in Education (2024) | Two-stage pipeline (gen + extract) | STEM secondary/tertiary | Factual (Q-A pair) | No | Weak on higher-order conceptual Qs | High |
| Neural QG Survey IJCAI (2024) | Survey (Recurrent, Transformer, Generative) | Multi-domain | Multi-hop, Conceptual | No | AQG isolated from dialogue systems | Very High |
| Automating QG from Educational Text (2023) | Curriculum-aware PLM | Textbooks (multi-discipline) | Curriculum-sequenced | Partial (sequencing) | Lacks real-time learner response integration | Very High |
| AQG Using ML (2022) | LSTM → Transformer survey | English benchmarks | Factual | No | English-only; performance ceiling | Medium |
| Controllable Open-ended QG (2021) | Conditioned generation | Open-domain text | Open-ended, Evaluative | Partial (type control) | No learner model or feedback loop | Very High |
| Future of Learning: Gen AI (2024) | Conceptual / Vision paper | Broad educational AI | Socratic, Dialogic | Advocated (not implemented) | No implementation; risks discussed but not addressed | Very High |
