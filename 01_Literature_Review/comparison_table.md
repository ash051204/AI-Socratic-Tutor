# Comparative Analysis of Existing Approaches

| Study | Approach Used | Adaptivity | Limitation |
|-------|---------------|------------|------------|
| Automated Socratic Tutoring (2021) | Rule-based questioning | Low | Rigid templates |
| Conversational AI Tutors (2023) | Dialogue-based inquiry | Medium | Limited conceptual depth |
| Bloom-based Adaptive Models (2020) | Level-based progression | High | No LLM integration |

## Observed Research Gap

Most existing systems either focus on structured questioning or adaptive difficulty, but rarely combine Socratic methodology, LLM conversational ability, and multi-level cognitive progression into a unified framework.
